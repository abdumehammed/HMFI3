About Us

Harar credit and saving institution S.C has been established in accordance to proclamation No 40/1988 in order to give full support for micro and small scale business operators in harari regional state in harar town. It was founded by Harari regional state, 6 visionary organizations and 1 voluntary person totally 8 /eight/ share holder with initial capital of birr 1,000,000.00/one million /in august 18 1998, upon securing licensee from national bank of Ethiopia, Harar credit and saving institution S.C opened its door for services on the 8th of September 2005 only in one main office. Harar credit and saving institution S.C coined- its name from the capital of harari regional state. Currently Harar credit and saving institution S.C has 3/three/ branch offices,6 service delivery posts had opened, The capital of Harar credit and saving institution S.C increased from birr 1,000,000.00/one million/ to 10,000,000.00 /ten million/ and the no of share holders also increased from 8 to 12.since its establishment the institution has been giving mainly credit , saving and development for different services like third party fund management.

Harar credit and saving institution S.C started its operation in 2005, with the number of 12 Staffs (10 men’s and 2 women). the institution started its work with only two loan product service namly Group loan and MSE loans, At the end of the beginning year HMFI reached with a total number of 422 customers, having a total/ collection of saving amount birr 372,204.75 /three hundred seventy two thousand two hundred four and 75/100/ including both /compulsory and voluntary saving/ and a total outstanding loan amount of birr 1,672,181.81 /one million six hundred t seventy two thousand one hundred and eighty one and 81/100/ in two products namely Group loans and MSE loans .All works at that time has been done manually.

Harar credit and saving institution S.C has registered a gradual growth and achievement I all aspects of its operation since establishment, At the end of financial year as of June 30, 2017, Its total no of customers and the total amount of saving stood at 9590 and 52,679,807.38 respectively, and with a total outstanding loan balance of 102,103,402.23. And also providing more than 8 diversified loan product service. Currently HMFI employee reached 39.Its total paid-up capital grow to 10,000,000 from 1,000,000 in 10%

Following a strong demand for better service and products from all directions on the one hand, and a ground-breaking development in ICT, has replaced its manual system with the data base management system.
